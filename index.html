How Long Does It Take For Delivery?

The time it takes for pizza delivery can vary based on several factors: Resturant Location, Order Volume, Prepration Time, Traffic & Weather. Typically, pizza delivery takes anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour. Many restaurants aim for around 30 to 45 minutes for standard deliveries. If you’re curious about a specific place, they often provide estimated delivery times when you place your order.

Can I Order For Huge Parties?

Absolutely! Many pizza restaurants and delivery services offer options for large orders, which are perfect for parties. Here’s how you can do it: Contact the Resturant, Choose your Sizes, Place the Order in Advanced, Ask about Discounts.
